art + tech + activist co-op

Unglitch is an activist co-operative connecting technologists and creatives to promote digital civil liberties. We build educational, reproducible, agnostic experiences to keep the discussion around technology policy relevant and creative. Members should contributed to at least 2 projects and attended 1 meeting, please reach out for more information.
